My life

At two months old, I was a tiny and curious pup, tucked inside a backpack, excited to explore the world with my new family!

My first experience at the groomer's was amazing! I felt so pampered and new, and I couldn't wait to show off my new look!

I love to curl up on the couch and take a nap. I often end up in a mess of blankets and pillows, with my paws sticking out at awkward angles.

I'm always ready to nap, and I'm sure I look super cute while I do so! I look so peaceful and content, and I'm sure my humans can't resist my adorable sleeping face.

I love to nap on my back, with my paws in the air and my chin resting on the ground. I stretch out in a star shape, soaking up all the warmth and sunlight around me.
